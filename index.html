About Us

Rooted in faith and dedicated to sharing the teachings of Jesus Christ, our church has been a beacon in this community for over three decades. We actively propagate the Word of God, striving to make its transformative power known to all. Beyond spiritual nourishment, we extend a helping hand, providing for the tangible needs of those around us. More than just a congregation, we are a family – a place where you can truly belong, find support, and grow in your relationship with God and one another. Join us and discover a welcoming community built on love, faith, and service.
